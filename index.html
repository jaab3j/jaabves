Phumisit 'Jaab' Veskijkul is a recent graduate from Middlebury College, where he pursued a joint major in Architecture and Environmental Studies and a minor in French and Francophone Studies. His background in architecture and design, combined with his fluency in Thai and English and intermediate level of French and Chinese, has allowed him to explore and excel in various creative fields, including content creation, branding, and acting.

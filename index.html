Bio: (he/him) Phumisit “Jaab” Veskijkul is a dual-degree candidate in the Master of Landscape Architecture + Urbanism and Master of Urban Planning programs at the University of Southern California. He earned a B.A. from Middlebury College in Environmental Studies and Architectural Studies. Raised in Chiang Mai, Thailand, his work explores water, climate-responsive urbanism, and the cultural edges between city, ecology, and infrastructure. Alongside design and planning, Jaab brings a multidisciplinary creative background including acting (notably in the Thai TV series Y-Destiny), visual storytelling, and media work which informs his interest in how landscapes carry narrative, identity, and everyday meaning.
